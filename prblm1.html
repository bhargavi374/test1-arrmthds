problem-1 Product Discount Scenario: Imagine you work at an e-commerce company where discounts are applied to customer purchases. Your code will help automate this calculation. Problem: Given a total purchase amount, calculate the discount and print the final amount. Input: An integer totalAmount, representing the total purchase amount. Conditions: If totalAmount > 500, apply a 20% discount. If totalAmount is between 200 and 500, apply a 10% discount. If totalAmount < 200, no discount is applied. Expected Output: The final amount after applying the discount. Example: Input: totalAmount=600 Output: 480 (600 - 20% of 600)
